# Data use and attribution

Academic Door Journals is an independent, non-profit academic information
project for Chinese readers.

- Paper titles, abstracts, author names, journal metadata, DOI records, and
  article links originate from their publishers and public metadata services.
- Rights in publisher-provided text remain with the corresponding publishers
  and authors.
- Academic Door does not redistribute paper PDFs or paywalled full text.
- Chinese translations and editorial organization are AI-assisted and may
  contain errors. Formal citation and research judgment must rely on the
  original source.
- Each public record should retain its source URL, retrieval time, and field
  provenance.

The MIT license in this repository applies to the software, not to third-party
journal content.
